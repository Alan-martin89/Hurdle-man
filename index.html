Hurdle Man

Jump into Fun with Hurdle Man

Enjoy the thrill and challenge of playing Hurdle Man, the fun game built using Scratch3 and HTMLify. Developed for mobile and desktop, it's the perfect game to introduce your kids to gaming!

About

Easy to learn & master
Hurdle Man is designed for ages 3+ and is simple enough for even the youngest of gamers to pick up and master quickly! With computer controls involving just a single spacebar press, or a simple tap on mobile devices, it's easy for anyone to play

A small game that packs a lot of fun
With version 1.0, you get an exciting game full of entertainment and challenge. Jump over hurdles with your character and have fun in this small but exciting game!

Controls

    Desktop
  • Spacebar To Jump
    Mobile
  • Touch Character To Jump

Designed for mobility & convenience

Hurdle Man is designed with mobile-first approach, so you can get the same gaming experience on mobile devices. The game is optimized for both mobile and desktop devices so you can play easily on any device!
